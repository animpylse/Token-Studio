Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2, 2026

1. No Data Collection & Zero Backend

Token Studio is designed with transparency and sovereignty at its core. The application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
• Zero Backend: We do not maintain any servers for storing user profiles, activity logs, or private keys.
• Local-First: All transaction history and application state are stored exclusively on your local device.

2. Information Processed Automatically

To provide high-density NFT management, certain technical information is processed temporarily through your use of the functionally:

  • IP Address: Your IP address is processed temporarily by network infrastructure to establish a secure connection between your device and our RPC endpoints. We do not log or use your IP address for marketing.
  • Public Blockchain Data: Your digital asset wallet address and associated public on-chain data are public by design on the Solana network. This data remains outside our control and cannot be altered or deleted.

3. Third-Party Service Providers (RPC)

Token Studio connects directly to third-party RPC providers to fetch live blockchain data:
• Helius: Used for high-speed metadata and compressed NFT (cNFT) proof operations.
• Solana RPCs: Used for standard blockchain queries (Mainnet/Devnet).
These providers may see your public wallet address as part of standard blockchain communication, but no other identifiable information is shared with them.

4. Seed Vault & Security

Token Studio utilizes the Solana Seed Vault API for all signing operations. Your private keys and recovery phrases never leave the secure enclave of your device. Token Studio has zero access to your private keys at any time.

5. Global Rights & GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain rights under the GDPR, including the right to access or rectify your data.

Important Immutability Caveat: Data recorded on the Solana blockchain (such as your transaction history) is immutable by design. We are technically unable to alter, delete, or "forget" on-chain records.

6. Contact

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via our official Discord Community Server or X (Twitter) handle.
