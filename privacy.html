Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 7, 2026

1. Overview

Token Studio is built on the principles of decentralization and user sovereignty. The application is designed to be fully privacy-focused, ensuring that you remain in control of your data and identity at all times.

2. Data Collection

Token Studio does not collect, store, or process any personal information. This includes, but is not limited to:
• Email addresses or names
• Wallet addresses or transaction history
• Device information or hardware identifiers
• IP addresses (beyond temporary network routing)
No user accounts exist, and we do not maintain a backend database for user activity.

3. Local Processing

All application logic and operations occur locally on your device or directly on public blockchain networks. We do not transmit your data to any central server for processing or storage.

4. Third-Party Services

To provide high-performance asset management, Token Studio interacts with specialized decentralized and third-party infrastructure:
• Irys & Arweave: For decentralized permanent storage.
• IPFS (via Pinata): For distributed asset hosting.
• Solana RPC Providers (including Helius): For live blockchain data.
These services operate independently and may process data according to their own policies. Users interact with these infrastructure providers directly via blockchain requests initiated from their device.

5. No Tracking

The application is committed to a tracker-free experience. Token Studio uses no cookies, no analytics platforms, and no tracking technologies. Your activity remains private and local to your device.

6. Data Security

Security is managed by the decentralized architecture of the Solana blockchain and the secure enclave of the Seed Vault wallet. Token Studio never has access to your private keys or sensitive data, and it does not store such information.

7. Children’s Privacy

Token Studio is not directed at children under the age of 13. It does not knowingly collect, solicit, or process any data from children.

8. On-Chain Immutability

Data recorded on the Solana blockchain (such as your transaction history, minting activity, or burns) is immutable by design. The developer does not control the Solana network and is technically unable to alter, delete, or "forget" any on-chain records. These actions are permanent and publicly accessible.

9. Changes to Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time to reflect changes in our technical practices or the evolving regulatory landscape. Continued use of the application constitutes your acceptance of the revised Policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the official Discord Community Server or X (Twitter) handle.
